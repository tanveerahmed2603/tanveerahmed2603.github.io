About Me

Tanveer Ahmed is a senior student at Baruch College majoring in Computer Information system. He is originally from Dhaka, Bangladesh. He graduated from New Town high school in 2013. He also holds an Associated Degree from CUNY LaGuardia Community College.

In summer 2019, He completed a summer internship from United Services Automobile Association (USAA). This is a San Antonio-based Fortune 100 diversified financial services group of companies including a Texas Department of Insurance-regulated reciprocal inter-insurance exchange and subsidiaries offering banking, investing, and insurance to people and families who serve, or served, in the United States Armed Forces.

In 2006, he joined Bangladesh National Cricket Institute to become a professional cricketer. He played for both Under 13 and Under 19 level of the national league until his migration to the United States.

Apart from playing Cricket, traveling is one of his addiction. During 2015-2019, he traveled 11 different countries including India, United Arab Emirates, Canada, Qatar, and Nepal.
